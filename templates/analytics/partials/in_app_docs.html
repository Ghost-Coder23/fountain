Help & Documentation — Quick Guide

Friendly, non-technical instructions to help you perform common tasks in the system. Use the sidebar Docs link to return to this page anytime.

Quick Start — Where to begin
  • Check the Dashboard first — it shows the most important school information at a glance (students, attendance, fees).
  • Use the sidebar to navigate: Academics, Attendance, Fees, Results, Messages, and Settings are grouped by area.
  • If you get stuck, click Docs in the sidebar or contact your school administrator.
1) Analytics & Dashboards

Dashboards are role-based — the information you see depends on your role (Headmaster, Admin, Secretary, Teacher, Parent, Student).

  • Headmaster / Admin: School-level summaries and quick actions for finance and approvals.
  • Secretary / Senior: Operational tools like the invoice list, payment recording, and attendance workflows.
  • Teacher: Your assigned classes, quick links to enter marks and view attendance for your lessons.
  • Parent / Student: View performance summaries, attendance, and outstanding fee balances.
2) Schools & Roles (Who can do what)

Roles control what you can see and do. If you need access to something you can’t reach, ask your Headmaster or Administrator to change your role.

  • Headmaster / Admin: Full access — can approve results, manage fees, and create school-wide announcements.
  • Secretary: Day-to-day operations — generate invoices, record payments, manage expenses.
  • Teacher: Enter and submit student marks, take attendance for classes.
  • Parent / Student: View student-specific information like reports and fee statements.
3) Academics — Students, Classes & Subjects

Common tasks:

  • Enroll a student: Use the Students page in the Academics section. Click New / Enroll and fill the simple form (name, admission number, class).
  • Create classes/sections: Use Class Levels and Classes to organize students. Teachers are assigned to classes or subjects to manage results and attendance.
  • Tip: Keep student and class data current — many other pages (fees, results) use this information.
4) Results — Entering and Approving Marks

A typical workflow:

  1. Teacher: Open Enter Results, pick your class and subject, and fill marks for students.
  2. Save or submit the results. Saved results are visible to you; submit when ready for review.
  3. Headmaster/Admin: Review Submitted results and Approve them. Once approved, the term is locked (only admins can change).

If marks do not appear where expected, check that you are assigned to the correct class/subject and that the current term/session is active.

5) Attendance — Marking and Reports
  • Take attendance: Go to Attendance → Mark Attendance. Choose class and date and record present / absent.
  • Finalize: If your school uses finalization, click Finalize so the attendance becomes official.
  • Reports: Attendance Reports show daily and weekly trends for the school.
6) Fees & Invoices — Simple non-technical guide

This covers creating invoices, searching, recording payments, and common scenarios.

6.1 View, search and filter invoices
  • Open Invoices from the sidebar.
  • Use the search box to find an invoice by student name, invoice number, or admission number.
  • Use the Status filter to show unpaid / partial / paid / overdue invoices.
6.2 Create an invoice
  1. Click New from the Invoices page.
  2. Select the student, fee structure (or enter amount), and billing period. Click Save.
  3. The invoice will be created and visible in the list. It gets an invoice number automatically.
6.3 Record a payment (step-by-step)
  1. Open the invoice (click View).
  2. On the right, use Record Payment. Enter amount, choose currency and method, and add a reference if available.
  3. Click Confirm Payment. The invoice balance will update; overpayments become student credit automatically.
6.4 Common fee scenarios (examples)
  • Student overpays: System stores the extra as Student Credit which can be applied to future invoices.
  • Invoice needs correction: If the invoice has no confirmed payments, an admin/headmaster/secretary can Void & Replace — this keeps an audit trail and creates a new editable invoice.
  • Missing invoice for a month: Use Payment Settings → Run now, or create the invoice manually from the Invoices page.
6.5 Student fee statement

Open a student profile and click Student Fee Statement to view all invoices, payments and credits for that student.

7) Expenses
  • Add expense categories (e.g., utilities, supplies).
  • Record expenses with amount, date and category so the finance dashboard reflects real spending.
8) Messaging & Announcements
  • Messages: Use Messages to communicate privately with teachers, parents or staff.
  • Announcements: Create school-wide announcements (Admins/Headmasters can send to everyone or selected groups).
9) Live Activity Feed

The small box at the bottom-right shows recent events (invoices voided, replacements created, announcements). It updates automatically. If you want only new items, watch for the newest timestamp at the top of the feed.

10) Offline & Mobile (Install the App)
  • The system supports working offline in a limited way. If you see an offline banner you can continue working; actions will sync when you reconnect.
  • To get faster access, install the app using the Install button or use your browser Add to Home Screen.
11) Settings & Config
  • School Settings: Update school name, logo and basic preferences (Headmaster / Admin only).
  • Payment Settings: Configure how invoices are created and the payment methods you accept.
12) If something goes wrong — simple troubleshooting
  • If a page shows no data, try refreshing the page or check your role — many pages are role-restricted.
  • If a search returns no results, try partial terms (e.g., last name only) or check the billing period filter.
  • For payment or invoicing issues, check for confirmed payments on the invoice and the Student Credit balance on the student statement.
  • If an action fails, note the steps you took and contact the school admin with those details.
13) Common how-to scenarios (short)
A) Create an invoice and record payment
  1. Invoices → New → fill student and amount → Save.
  2. Open invoice → Record Payment → enter amount → Confirm Payment.
B) Void an incorrect invoice and create a replacement
  1. Open the invoice → Click Void & Replace (available to Headmaster/Admin/Secretary) → enter reason → Confirm.
  2. The original is kept for audit and a new editable invoice appears in the list.
C) Enter and approve results
  1. Teacher: Results → Enter Results → fill marks → Submit.
  2. Headmaster/Admin: Pending Approvals → Review → Approve.
